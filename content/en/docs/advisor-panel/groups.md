Groups help advisors organize students into cohorts for assignment and communication.

## A) Open Groups

1. In sidebar, click Groups.
2. Wait for groups list to load.

## B) Create Group (Click-by-Click)

1. Click Create Group.
2. Enter group name.
3. Enter optional description.
4. Select student members.
5. Click Save.
6. Confirm new group appears in list.

## C) Edit Group

1. Open target group.
2. Click Edit.
3. Update name/description/members.
4. Save changes.
5. Confirm updated values in list/detail.

## D) Delete Group

1. Open group actions.
2. Click Delete.
3. Confirm deletion prompt.
4. Verify group is removed.

## E) Best Practice for Group Strategy

- Create groups by grade, track, or campaign objective.
- Keep names consistent and searchable.
- Review membership regularly.

## Common Advisor Questions

Q: I cannot add a student to group.
A: Check permission scope or reload student source list.

Q: Delete action is unavailable.
A: Permission may be restricted for sub-advisor role.

Q: Group saved but member count is wrong.
A: Re-open edit dialog and verify selected members before save.
