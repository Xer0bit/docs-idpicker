## Access Shared Resources

1. Open Resources from navigation.
2. Browse advisor-shared files.

## Find Specific Material

1. Use search by title/description/file name.
2. Apply filters by type, size, and visibility.
3. Sort results as needed.

## Open or Download File

1. Click resource item.
2. Open preview or download.
