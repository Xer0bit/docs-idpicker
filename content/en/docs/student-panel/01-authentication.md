## Login with Email and Password

1. Open [Access URLs](../getting-started/access-urls/).
2. Visit the Main Platform Login URL.
3. Enter email and password.
4. Click Sign In.
5. Verify dashboard load.

## Login with Google OAuth

1. On login page, click Continue with Google.
2. Select account and approve access.
3. Verify redirect to Student Dashboard.

## Password Reset

1. Click Forgot Password.
2. Enter registered email.
3. Open reset link from email.
4. Set new password and login again.
