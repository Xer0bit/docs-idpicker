## Start Mentor Chat

1. From dashboard, click Engage Your Mentor.
2. Type your question.
3. Continue conversation with follow-up prompts.

## Get Guided Advice

1. Open Guided Advice from mentor panel.
2. Review step-by-step recommendations.
