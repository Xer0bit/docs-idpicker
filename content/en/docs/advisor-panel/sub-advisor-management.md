## Create Sub-Advisor

1. Open Advisor Settings or Team Management.
2. Click Create Sub-Advisor.
3. Fill required fields: name, email, scope, and assigned student group.
4. Save and send invitation.

## Manage Permissions

1. Open sub-advisor record.
2. Configure visibility scope.
3. Save and test effective access.

## Deactivate Sub-Advisor

1. Open sub-advisor account.
2. Set status to inactive.
3. Verify access is revoked.
