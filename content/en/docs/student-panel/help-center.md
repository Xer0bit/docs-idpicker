Sometimes things break or you encounter a feature you do not understand. The Help Center is your direct line to the IDPicker platform support team.

---

## Opening a New Support Ticket

1. In the sidebar, click **Help Center**.
2. Make sure the **New Ticket** tab is selected.
3. Click the **Subject** line and type a brief, clear summary (e.g., *Cannot download personality report*).
4. Select the matching **Category** (Technical, Account, Billing, Feature, General, or Other).
5. Select a **Priority** (Low, Medium, High, Urgent).
   - *Note: Please only use Urgent for complete account blockers (like being totally unable to log in).*
6. In the **Description** box, provide as much detail as possible.
7. Click **Submit** and wait for the success flash.

## How to Write a Ticket That Gets Fixed Fast

The support team wants to help, but they cannot see your screen. Provide these details to speed up resolution:

- **Exactly what page or module** you were on.
- The **exact text** of any error message shown.
- **What you clicked** before the error happened.
- What you *expected* to happen, vs what *actually* happened.

## Tracking Your Submitted Tickets

Once submitted, you can monitor the status of your request without sending duplicate emails.

1. Click the **My Tickets** tab at the top of the Help Center.
2. Find the issue in your list.
3. Review the **Status** badge to understand current progress:

| Status Badge | What it Means |
|---|---|
| **Pending** | Received, sitting in the queue waiting for an agent |
| **In Progress** | A support agent is actively investigating right now |
| **Resolved** | The agent has implemented a fix or provided an answer |
| **Closed** | The issue is fully completed and archived |

If an agent replies requiring more info, you will be notified!

## Common Questions

| Situation | What to do |
|---|---|
| I clicked submit but got a red error | Check that you actually wrote a Subject and Description. Both are required! |
| I submitted my ticket but my list is empty | You are probably still on the *New Ticket* tab. Switch back to *My Tickets* |
| What category do I pick for login issues? | Select *Account*, unless you are specifically facing a payment/subscription issue, then pick *Billing* |
