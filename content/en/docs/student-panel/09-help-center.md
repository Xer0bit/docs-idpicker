## Submit a Ticket

1. Open Help Center.
2. Click Submit New Ticket.
3. Fill subject, category, priority, description.
4. Submit and note ticket ID.

## Track Existing Tickets

1. Open My Tickets.
2. Check status updates.
3. Open ticket for details.
