Student Detail, Journey Map, and Advisor Chat

How to monitor your child’s progress, export their Journey Map, and communicate with advisors

Once you open a linked student’s profile from your dashboard, you gain access to powerful tracking tools and direct communication lines with their educational team.

1. Reviewing Progress and Insights

At the top of the student detail page, you will see their Basic Profile (name, age, grade). Below this, the page is broken down into insight blocks:

  • Assessment Progress: Quickly see which required psychological or educational assessments your child has completed, and which are still pending.
  • Recommendations Summary: A high-level view of the career paths or universities the AI has suggested for them based on their results.
  • Mentorship Activity: See if your child has been utilizing the AI mentors or checking in with their human advisor.

2. Generating the Journey Map

The Journey Map is a comprehensive, exportable report that synthesizes your child’s entire profile into a single document. It is highly useful for college applications or external counseling.

  1. Scroll to the Journey Map section on the detail page.
  2. Select the specific Data Points you want included in the report. You can check or uncheck categories like:
    • Demographics & Education
    • Psychological Traits
    • Career Ambitions
    • Social, Family & Health Metrics
  3. Click Preview to see a structural mockup of the data before committing to the download.
  4. Once you are satisfied with the included data, click Generate & Download Journey Map.
  5. The system will process the file (this takes a few seconds) and open a secure PDF download link in a new tab.

3. Chatting with the Advisor

If your child’s school has enabled Family Chat, you can communicate directly with their assigned advisor from this page.

  1. Locate the Advisor Chat panel on the student detail page.
  2. A red badge will indicate if you have unread messages.
  3. Click to open the secure chat thread.
  4. You can read past messages, ask questions, and coordinate support strategies directly through the platform.

4. Managing Subscription Plans

If IDPicker is offered to you as a direct-to-consumer product (rather than through a school), you control the billing.

  1. Scroll to the Subscription area.
  2. Review your child’s current active plan tier.
  3. Compare available upgrades (e.g., unlocking full PDF reports or unlimited mentor chats).
  4. Click to initiate the secure stripe/checkout flow to upgrade their account instantly.

Common Questions

Situation What to do
The Journey Map preview is totally empty You must select at least one data point checkbox (like “Education”) before the preview will generate
The Journey map downloaded, but pages are blank Your child may not have completed enough assessments to populate those specific sections yet
I see a high unread count, but no new messages in the Advisor Chat Try refreshing the page. In rare cases, the unread count may reflect messages sent strictly to your child (Student-Advisor thread) rather than the Parent-Advisor thread
