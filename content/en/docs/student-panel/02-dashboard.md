## Dashboard Purpose

The dashboard centralizes mentor access, assessments, reports, and upgrade flow.

## Steps

1. Log in as student.
2. Review top cards and pillar widgets.
3. Open AI Mentor from dashboard CTA.
4. Open each core pillar and confirm page navigation.
5. Open reports and subscription CTA.
