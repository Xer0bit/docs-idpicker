Advisor Panel

Complete click-by-click advisor operations manual

Use this section as the primary operational manual for advisor and sub-advisor workflows.

Who Should Use This Guide

  • Advisor users managing students, groups, and operations
  • Sub-advisor users with delegated permissions
  • Support/QA teams validating advisor-side flows

Suggested Reading Order

  1. Login and dashboard overview
  2. Students and bulk import
  3. Groups and membership control
  4. Forms and responses
  5. Resources and distribution
  6. Support tickets
  7. Notifications and targeting
  8. Certificates and application documents
  9. Sub-advisor management and permissions
  10. Enrollment requests, family chat, and assessments
  11. Profile, prompts, and interests

Core Features & Responsibilities

  • Manage student accounts and parent-linked visibility
  • Organize cohorts with groups and assignments
  • Create and monitor forms/surveys
  • Share resources and track support tickets
  • Send broadcasts to students and groups
  • Manage document review and certificate operations
  • Delegate work through sub-advisor accounts

Screenshot Checklist

  1. Advisor login page and successful dashboard
  2. Students table with filters and detail open
  3. Bulk import file upload and preview
  4. Group create/edit dialog with student assignment
  5. Forms list with status filters and actions
  6. Resources page with upload and assignment
  7. Support ticket dashboard with status update
  8. Notification creation modal with target selection
  9. Certificates page and application documents review
  10. Sub-advisor permissions and assignment view
  11. Enrollment request approve/deny actions
  12. Family chat tabs and conversation pane
  13. Assessment management workflow
  14. Profile, prompts, and interests pages
