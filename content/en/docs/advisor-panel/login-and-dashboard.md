## Advisor Login

1. Open advisor login URL from [Access URLs](../getting-started/access-urls/).
2. Enter advisor credentials.
3. Click Sign In.

## Dashboard Navigation

1. Review assigned student groups.
2. Open student profile summary.
3. Check alerts, pending actions, and activity timeline.
