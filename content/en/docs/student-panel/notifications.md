IDPicker keeps you informed through a built-in notification system. Whenever your advisor sends you a message, assigns you a form, or an action requires your attention, a notification appears in your inbox so nothing falls through the cracks.

---

## 1. Opening Your Notification Inbox

1. Look for the **Bell icon** in the top navigation bar.
2. A red badge on the bell shows how many unread notifications you have.
3. Click the bell to open the **Notification Dropdown** — this shows your five most recent messages.
4. Click **View All Notifications** at the bottom of the dropdown to open the full inbox page.

## 2. Reading a Notification

1. In the full notifications list, unread items appear with a highlighted background or a blue dot indicator.
2. Click any notification row to open it.
3. The detail view shows:
   - **Title** of the notification
   - **Full message body** — may include instructions or links to take action
   - **Sender** (e.g., your advisor's name, or *IDPicker System*)
   - **Timestamp** — when it was sent
4. After you open it, the notification is automatically marked as **Read** and the unread badge count decreases.

---

## 3. Marking All as Read

If you have many unread notifications and want to clear the badge quickly:

1. On the full notifications list page, click **Mark All as Read**.
2. All notification rows are instantly marked as read.
3. The red badge on the bell icon disappears.

> **Note:** Marking notifications as read does not delete them. They remain in your list for reference.

## 4. Types of Notifications

| Type | What it means | What to do |
|---|---|---|
| **Advisor Message** | Your advisor sent you a note or update | Read the message and reply in the Chat module if needed |
| **Form Assigned** | An advisor or admin has added a new form for you to complete | Click the notification to go directly to the form and fill it in |
| **Assessment Reminder** | A scheduled or overdue assessment needs your attention | Click through to open the assessment and complete it |
| **Parent Link Request Received** | A parent has requested to view your account | Go to your Account Settings to approve or deny the link request |
| **Certificate Issued** | Your advisor has issued you a new certificate | Find it in the Certificates and Documents section to download |
| **Application Document Update** | Your advisor reviewed and updated the status of a document you uploaded | Check your Application Documents section for feedback |

---

## 5. Acting on a Notification

Most notifications include a direct action link inside the message body. Click the link to jump straight to the relevant page — for example, clicking a *Form Assigned* notification takes you directly to the form's fill screen.

If a notification does not have a direct link, use the sidebar navigation to go to the relevant module manually.

---

## Common Questions

| Situation | What to do |
|---|---|
| My bell shows a badge count but I cannot find unread notifications | Open the full inbox and look for the blue dot or highlighted row. The count may include system-level notices that appear without a visible highlight in all browsers |
| I accidentally marked everything as read before seeing a message | The notifications are still in your list. Scroll through and look for any you have not acted on yet |
| I am not receiving notifications about my forms | Check with your advisor to confirm the form was assigned to your account specifically. Also verify your notifications settings have not been muted |
