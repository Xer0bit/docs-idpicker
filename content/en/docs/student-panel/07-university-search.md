## Search Programs

1. Open University Search.
2. Enter university or program keyword.
3. Review matching results.

## Filter Results

Use filters for:
- Field of study
- Degree type
- Language

## Open Program Details

1. Click a program card.
2. Review tuition, duration, country, and language.
