Student panel supports English and Turkish.

## Switch Language (Normal Case)

1. Open sidebar.
2. Click language switcher.
3. Select target language.
4. Wait for UI text refresh.
5. Navigate to another page to confirm persistence.

## Switch Language During In-Progress Assessment

If an assessment is in progress, a confirmation dialog can appear.

1. Select new language.
2. Read warning: in-progress assessment may be reset.
3. Click Cancel to keep current language and progress.
4. Or click Confirm to switch language and reset in-progress state.
5. Page reloads with selected language.

## Validation Checklist

After language switch, verify:

- Sidebar labels are translated.
- Dashboard headings are translated.
- Form/assessment labels reflect selected language.

## Common Student Questions

Q: Why did language switch ask for confirmation?
A: You had an in-progress personalized assessment.

Q: Why was assessment progress cleared?
A: You confirmed language change while progress-reset warning was active.

Q: Some labels stayed old language.
A: Refresh page once and verify language persisted.
